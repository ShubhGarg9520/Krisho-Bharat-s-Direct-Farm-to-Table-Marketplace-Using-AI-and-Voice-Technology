Middlemen Exploitation

Multi-layer intermediaries drain farmer profits and inflate prices for consumers by 200–400%.

📡

Rural Accessibility Gap

Modern e-commerce platforms are inaccessible to rural farmers with language and tech barriers.

🥬

Massive Produce Wastage

Delayed supply chains cause huge spoilage. Farmers lose income; consumers lose freshness.

💰

No Price Transparency

Farmers have no visibility into market prices, leaving them at the mercy of local traders.

Solution
Key Features
🚜

Dual-Role Ecosystem

Separate portals for Suppliers (farmers) and Consumers with tailored dashboards and workflows.

💬

Real-Time Negotiation

Socket.io-powered direct chat between buyers and farmers with Firestore message persistence.

🤖

Gemini AI Assistant

Language-aware AI (Hindi/English) for product advice, auto-categorization and smart recommendations.

🎙️

Voice Guide (Multilingual)

Built-in voice instructor in Hindi & English — making the app fully accessible for rural farmers.

💳

Secure Payments

Razorpay integration with automated multi-supplier split payouts and order reconciliation.

⭐

Ratings & Reviews

Verified buyer reviews, star ratings and supplier reputation management from the dashboard.
